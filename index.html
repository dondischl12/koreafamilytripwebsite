dad mode

History/audio/book recs for the cultural parts.

These are not homework. They are “listen on the flight / read a chapter before the palace” context boosts.

Podcasts / audio

  • In Our Time — The Korean Empire: good for understanding Korea’s late-Joseon-to-modern transition.
  • The History of Korea podcast: slower, deeper chronological background if your dad wants the long arc.
  • Amateur Traveler — Places to Visit in Seoul: practical orientation, including historic sites around Seoul.

Books

  • A Brief History of Korea — best general pre-trip history read.
  • Korea: The Impossible Country — modern Korea / economy / society context.
  • The Korean War: A History — heavier but useful for 20th-century context.
  • Pachinko — historical fiction lens on Korean identity/diaspora, not Seoul-specific.
