Jun 24

Arrival → RETTRE → Seongsu city-vibes → Itaewon/Hannam night

Day 1 · Seongsu Birthday Energy
Land soft. Go cool.

Zone: Mondrian Itaewon → Seoul Forest / Seongsu → Itaewon or Hannam near hotel

11:00–12:30 · Arrive / drop bags / reset

Hotel baseLow stress

Get into Seoul, drop bags at Mondrian, hydrate, and do not turn arrival into a marathon. The day becomes special at 3 PM, so the first move is just getting everyone settled.

Mondrian MapsRoute to RETTRE

3:00 · RETTRE Seoul Forest brainwave perfume

Booked4 peopleArrive early

This is the birthday anchor: a custom scent based on memory and brainwave measurement. Pick one meaningful photo on the phone beforehand. Go to the exact Seoul Forest branch — not Seongsu, not Yeonmujang, not Hongdae.

Location2F, 45-1 Seoul Forest 2-gil, Seongdong-gu, Seoul
Do beforeFavorite one meaningful photo in the phone gallery. Family, travel, a place, a memory — anything with feeling.
RETTRE Google MapsRETTRE Official

4:00–8:00 · Seongsu: cafés, retail theater, pop-ups

Main zoneBrooklyn of Seoul

After RETTRE, stay in Seongsu. This is where Seoul feels like the future of retail: café warehouses, pop-ups, concept stores, fragrance, eyewear, desserts, and people dressed like they are already in a magazine.

Must-hit shopsHaus Nowhere, Gentle Monster, Tamburins, Nudake, LCDC Seoul, Point of View Seongsu.
CafésCafe Onion Seongsu, Daelim Changgo, Lowkey Coffee, Rain Report if nearby.
Haus NowhereCafe OnionLCDC SeoulPoint of View

After 8 · Near-hotel night: pick A or B

Option A: Easy Itaewon nightMondrian → Itaewon main street → dessert/café → back. Best if everyone is tired but wants a little Seoul night energy.
Option B: Stylish Hannam nightMondrian → Hannam café streets → Leeum / Vinyl & Plastic area → back. Better if everyone still has energy and wants design-y night vibes.
Itaewon walkHannam cafésLeeum areaVinyl & Plastic

Jun 25

Shopping chaos → Mongvely KBBQ → DDP night

Day 2 · Shopping + BBQ + Futuristic Night
Buy everything. Grill everything.

Zone: Namdaemun → Myeongdong → Mongvely → Dongdaemun / DDP

10:30–1:00 · Namdaemun + department store drift

MarketCheap finds

Start with Namdaemun Market for socks, accessories, snacks, souvenirs, random finds, and chaotic local shopping. Then drift toward Shinsegae and Lotte for the polished department-store contrast.

NamdaemunShinsegae MainLotte Main

1:00–5:30 · Myeongdong shopping chaos

Olive YoungDaisoStreet shops

Beauty shopping, cheap stores, street food, flagship shops, and gifts. This is the day to go full haul mode. Keep lunch light because the KBBQ dinner is the anchor.

Must-hitOlive Young Myeongdong Global Town, Daiso Myeongdong Station, Myeongdong Walking Street.
OptionalNike, Adidas, ABC Mart, random skincare stores, street food snacks, photo booths.
Olive YoungDaisoWalking Street

6:00–8:00 · Mongvely KBBQ

Proper grillBeef + porkAYCE

Mongvely is the casual group KBBQ call: real table grill, all-you-can-eat beef and pork, right in Myeongdong. Go a little early if you can, because this is the obvious dinner anchor after shopping.

Mongvely MapsMongvely Official

8:15–10:30 · DDP / Dongdaemun night

Option BFuturistic Seoul

This is the better ending. After KBBQ, go to Dongdaemun Design Plaza for the night architecture, lights, photos, and late-city energy. If the group still wants to walk, route through Euljiro or Cheonggyecheon.

DDP MapsCheonggyecheonDoota Mall

Jun 26

Temple → COEX → Gangnam cafés → Han River bikes

Day 3 · River Day
Calm start. Big sunset.

Zone: Bongeunsa / COEX → Sinsa or Apgujeong → Banpo Hangang Park

10:30–1:30 · Bongeunsa + COEX / Starfield Library

Quick templeAC blockBook Fair option

Bongeunsa gives the quick temple moment without turning the day into homework. Then cross into COEX for Starfield Library, mall wandering, and optionally the Seoul International Book Fair if dad wants the event stop.

Food anchorPlantude COEX for a strong vegan meal. Everyone else can split into the COEX food court/mall options.
Event optionSeoul International Book Fair runs June 24–28 at COEX Halls A & B1.
BongeunsaStarfield LibraryPlantudeBook Fair

2:00–5:00 · Sinsa / Garosu-gil or Apgujeong

CafésBoutiquesGangnam style

Pick one lane: Sinsa/Garosu-gil for boutiques and cafés, or Apgujeong/Dosan Park for more polished fashion, people-watching, and flagship-store energy. This is a different shopping feel from Myeongdong.

Garosu-gilApgujeong RodeoDosan Park cafés

5:30–9:30 · Banpo Hangang bikes + Rainbow Fountain

SunsetBike rideRiver picnic

This is the summer Seoul moment. Arrive before sunset, rent bikes, ride along the river, grab convenience-store snacks/ramen, then stay for Banpo Bridge Rainbow Fountain and Some Sevit night lights.

Banpo ParkRainbow FountainSome Sevit

Jun 27

Mangwon → Yeonnam → Hongdae weekend finale

Day 4 · Last-Night Chaos
Local eats. Final night.

Zone: Mangwon Market → Yeonnam cafés → Hongdae buskers / arcade / noraebang

11:30–2:30 · Mangwon Market + Mangridan-gil

Local marketCafésSnack crawl

Start local. Mangwon feels less tourist-obvious than Myeongdong and more casual than Gwangjang. Do snacks, bakeries, prop shops, and the Mangridan-gil café streets.

Mangwon MarketMangridan-gilOptional river

3:00–6:00 · Yeonnam cafés + shops

Soft landingPhoto boothsBoutiques

Move into Yeonnam before Hongdae gets fully loud. This is where you do cafés, little shops, Gyeongui Line Forest Park, photo booths, and a slower afternoon before the night hits.

Yeonnam CafésForest ParkVintage shops

6:30–late · Hongdae weekend finale

BuskersArcadeNoraebang

Saturday night in Hongdae should be packed and fun. Start with the street performers and walking streets, then do arcade/VR games, photo booths, and finish with noraebang if the group still has juice.

Hongdae Walking StreetVR SquareSU NoraebangArcades

Quick goodbye block before 11 AM departure

Departure · Micro plan

No stressBags firstNearby only
